What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us remember your preferences and improve your browsing experience.

How We Use Cookies

  • Essential: Cart functionality, login sessions, and security. Required for the site to function.
  • Analytics: We track page views and browsing patterns to improve our site performance and user experience.
  • Functional: Remember your preferences like currency, language, and recently viewed products.

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling certain cookies may affect site functionality (e.g., you won't be able to add items to your cart).

Third-Party Cookies

We use services like Google Analytics and Stripe (payment) which may set their own cookies. These are governed by their respective privacy policies.
