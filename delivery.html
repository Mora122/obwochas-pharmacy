Delivery Options

We offer flexible delivery options to suit your schedule. All orders are packed with care by our pharmacists and dispatched promptly.

🚚 Express Delivery: Get your order within 3 hours in select areas when you order before 7 pm. Perfect for urgent medication needs.

📦 Standard Delivery: Receive your order within 24-48 hours anywhere in Kenya. Free for orders above KSh 2,500/=.

🏪 Click & Collect: Order online and pick up at your nearest Obwocha's Pharmacy store — free and available within 2 hours.
