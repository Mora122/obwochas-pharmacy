❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Obwocha's Pharmacy

Home / FAQ
🕐 What are your store hours? ▼
Our stores are open Monday to Saturday from 7:00 AM to 9:00 PM, and Sunday & Public Holidays from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM. Hours may vary by location — check your nearest store for exact times.
🚚 How does delivery work? ▼
We offer nationwide delivery across Kenya. Express delivery (3 hours) is available in select areas for orders placed before 7 PM. Standard delivery takes 1-3 business days. Free delivery on orders above KSh 2,500.
💳 What payment methods do you accept? ▼
We accept M-Pesa, Visa, MasterCard, and Cash-on-Delivery for online orders. All major payment methods including mobile money, cards, and cash are accepted in-store.
📋 How do I submit a prescription online? ▼
You can upload a clear photo of your prescription on our Submit Prescription page. A registered pharmacist will review and prepare your order. You can choose to collect or have it delivered.
🔄 What is your return policy? ▼
We accept returns of unopened, non-prescription products within 7 days of purchase with a valid receipt. Prescription medicines cannot be returned once dispensed due to regulatory requirements.
✅ Are your products 100% genuine? ▼
Absolutely. All products sold at Obwocha's Pharmacy are sourced directly from manufacturers and authorized distributors. We guarantee 100% authenticity on every item.
🎁 How does the Obwocha's Health Club loyalty program work? ▼
Obwocha's Health Club rewards you with points on every purchase. Accumulate points and redeem them for discounts on future purchases. Sign up at any store or online for free.
💊 Can I speak to a pharmacist online? ▼
Yes! You can consult with our pharmacists via WhatsApp (0727747699), phone (0727747699), or through our online chat. Free professional advice available 7 days a week.
📍 How do I find my nearest Obwocha store? ▼
Visit our Store Locator page to find the Obwocha's Pharmacy nearest to you. We have 150+ stores across all 47 counties in Kenya.
