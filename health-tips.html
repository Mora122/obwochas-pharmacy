🩺 Health Tips

Expert advice from our pharmacists for a healthier you

Home / Health Tips
💊

Managing Your Medication

Always take medication at the same time each day. Use a pill organizer to avoid missed doses. Never double up on missed doses — consult your pharmacist.

🥗

Nutrition for Wellness

Eat a rainbow of fruits and vegetables daily. Include whole grains, lean proteins, and healthy fats. Limit processed foods and added sugars.

🏃

Stay Active

Adults need at least 150 minutes of moderate activity per week. Walking, cycling, and swimming are excellent low-impact options for all ages.

😴

Sleep Hygiene

Aim for 7-9 hours of quality sleep nightly. Maintain a consistent sleep schedule, avoid screens 1 hour before bed, and keep your bedroom cool and dark.

💧

Stay Hydrated

Drink at least 8 glasses of water daily. Increase intake in hot weather or during exercise. Herbal teas and fresh fruits also contribute to hydration.

🧘

Mental Wellness

Practice mindfulness or meditation for 10 minutes daily. Stay connected with loved ones. Don't hesitate to seek professional help when needed.
