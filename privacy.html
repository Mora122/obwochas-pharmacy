Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your data

Home / Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you place an order or create an account on Obwocha's Pharmacy, we collect: your name, phone number, email address, delivery address, payment information, and order history. We also collect browsing data through cookies.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to process and deliver orders, communicate about your purchases, send promotional offers (with consent), improve our services, and comply with legal obligations.

3. Data Protection

We implement industry-standard security measures including SSL encryption, secure payment gateways, and restricted data access. Your payment details are processed by Stripe and M-Pesa — we never store full card numbers.

4. Data Sharing

We never sell your personal data. We may share with delivery partners (name, address, phone) and payment processors (payment info) solely for order fulfillment.

5. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time. Contact us at info@obwochaspharmacy.co.ke or call 0727747699.

6. Cookies

We use essential cookies for cart functionality and analytics cookies to improve our site. You can disable cookies in your browser settings. See our Cookie Policy.
