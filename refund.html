Our Returns Policy

We want you to be fully satisfied with your purchase. Our returns process is simple and straightforward.

⏱️ Return Window: Items can be returned within 14 days of delivery or store purchase.

✅ Condition: Products must be unopened, unused, and in their original packaging (except for defective items).

🚫 Exclusions: For hygiene reasons, certain items (opened medicines, intimate products, face masks) cannot be returned once opened or used.

🔄
💰

Refund Process

💳 Online Orders: Refunds are processed to the original payment method within 5-7 business days after we receive the returned items.

🏪 In-Store Purchases: Return to any Obwocha's Pharmacy store with your receipt for an immediate exchange or refund.

📦 Damaged/Defective: Contact us within 24 hours of receiving your order. We'll arrange a replacement or full refund including delivery costs.

Email returns@obwochaspharmacy.co.ke or call 0727747699 to initiate a return.
